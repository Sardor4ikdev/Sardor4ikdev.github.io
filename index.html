Courses (Soon)

Our long term plan is to create affordable IT courses where students can study the basics of programming profoundly from school ages.

Read More

We have identified three main problems of far-lolcated schools which we would solve:
1. Lack of books and poor condition of existing ones
2. The scarcity of additional materials for self-education
3. Weak computer science lessons or even absence of them.

After the disclosure of these problems we brainstormed for possible solutions and came to these resolutions:
1. To get official school books from official sources(Ministry of Education) and create a web archive where any student can access to their needed books.
2. To create a list of links to free educational materials and useful tools.
3. To launch our own free courses where students can study the basics of IT profoundly from school ages.

It is obvious that startups will not launch without investments, so at this point we have two choices:
1. To find a philanthropist who would invest in us interest-free.
2. Approach venture funds/business angels.

Of course we would not stop developing our platform and adding new functions, we would update the books archive annualy and enlarge our list of useful materials as often as we can. It is just beginning. Now we are working on our free IT courses and meditating about how this can be realised.
